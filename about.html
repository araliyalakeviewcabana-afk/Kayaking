Our Story

A Legacy of Hospitality

For over 12 years, we have been crafting unforgettable resort and cabana experiences for guests visiting Udawalawe. Rooted in this deep passion for exceptional hospitality and our love for nature, we recently launched our exclusive kayaking tours to give adventurers a closer, more intimate bond with the breathtaking Udawalawe Lake.

Though our kayaking service began just a few months ago, our decade-long legacy ensures you receive world-class service, expert guidance, and uncompromising attention to safety. In this short time, we've already had the pleasure of guiding over 100 happy kayakers through these serene waters. We bring over 12 years of hospitality excellence directly to your water adventure.
